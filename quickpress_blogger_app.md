QuickPress – Blogger to Mobile App | Flutter News & Blog App (AdMob Ads Ready)
==============================================================================

QuickPress is a Flutter-based mobile app template that converts your Google Blogger blog into a fully functional Android app. Built with Google Blogger v3 API, AdMob monetization, and OneSignal push notifications, QuickPress is the perfect solution for bloggers, news apps, and content creators who want zero maintenance, fast deployment, and SEO-friendly performance.

Why QuickPress is the Best Blogger App Template
-----------------------------------------------

No hosting. No backend. No maintenance. QuickPress runs entirely on Google Blogger and uses official APIs, making it fast, secure, and reliable.
